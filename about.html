About Us

The Vault — Crestfield University Recreation Center was established in 2015 to provide students, staff, and faculty with a modern space for fitness, wellness, and community engagement. Its mission is to promote healthy lifestyles by offering accessible facilities, inclusive programs, and a welcoming environment where everyone can move, train, and belong.

Facility Access

Who can use The Vault and how to get access.

Students

Free Access

All registered students can use the facility at no extra cost. Simply present your valid student ID at the entrance.

Staff & Faculty

$75 / Semester

University staff and faculty can gain full access by registering and paying a semester fee.

* Access may be restricted during special events or maintenance periods.
